EKO

Hello,

EKO makes radio more accessible, as an integrated part of your everyday life.

When you are close it starts playing the latest Ekot broadcast. It adjusts the volume as you move closer or further away, pauses when you head in to the bathroom, and once you get back it resumes right where you left off.

EKO was created at #hackaway by @kalasjocke and @jede.
